# Google Ads Campaign — Joyful Zen Academy (Fall 2026 Tours)

**Goal:** Book tours / enrollment inquiries for the Academy (grades K–5, ages 6–10)
**Audience:** Parents in Burbank, Toluca Lake, Studio City, North Hollywood, Glendale searching for microschool / pod learning / alternative elementary
**Urgency:** 2 spots just opened for Fall 2026 (school year starts August). Enrolling + touring now.
**Landing page:** https://joyfulzenacademy.com/book-a-tour  (send ALL ad traffic here, not the homepage)

---

## Campaign Settings (recommended)

- **Campaign type:** Search
- **Bid strategy:** Maximize Conversions (switch to Maximize Conversions w/ target CPA after ~15 conversions)
- **Conversion action:** "Book a Tour" form submit (make sure this is set up in Google Ads → Goals)
- **Location targeting:** Radius — 8 miles around Burbank 91505, OR target these cities: Burbank, Toluca Lake, Studio City, North Hollywood, Glendale. Set to **"Presence: People in your targeted locations"** (not "interest").
- **Daily budget:** start $25–40/day; concentrate spend while spots are open
- **Ad schedule:** all day, but bump bids +20% weekdays 7–9am & 7–10pm (when parents research)
- **Networks:** Search only (uncheck Display & Search Partners to start)
- **Devices:** all; mobile usually wins for local

## Quick-launch checklist

1. Confirm **conversion tracking** fires on the Book a Tour "thank you" / submit. Without this, you're flying blind.
2. Send **100% of ad traffic to `/book-a-tour`** — never the homepage. Fewer clicks to the goal = more tours.
3. Add all **negative keywords** before going live (saves real money week one).
4. Turn on **all extensions** — they're free and lift CTR meaningfully.
5. Set location to **"Presence: People in your targeted locations."**
6. Start at **$25–40/day**, check after 3–4 days, pause any keyword with clicks but zero form starts.
7. Because openings are limited, consider a tight **8-mile radius** first; widen only if volume is low.
