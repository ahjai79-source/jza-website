✦ A day in the life

Our daily rhythm

A gentle, predictable flow gives little ones the security to explore, learn, and shine. Here's how a joyful day unfolds.

9:00
Gentle Arrival

Soft music, warm hellos, and open invitations to play. We ease into the morning so every child feels settled, safe, and ready.

9:30
Creative Studio

Paint, playdough, and collage. Open-ended art where little hands explore textures, colors, and big ideas.

10:00
Circle Time

Songs, stories, weather, and our daily question on the rug, building language, listening, and a sense of belonging.

10:20
Nourish & Outdoor Play

A healthy snack to refuel, then outside we go! Running, climbing, and digging build strong bodies and brave hearts.

11:00
Mindful Movement

Gentle stretches, breathing, and balance games help little ones notice their bodies and find their calm.

11:15
Spanish Enrichment

¡Hola! Songs, colors, and playful words introduce a second language in the most natural, joyful way.

11:30
Montessori Work Cycle

Hands-on, self-directed discovery, children choose purposeful activities that grow focus, independence, and early skills.

12:00
Community Lunch

We share a meal family-style, practicing kindness, conversation, and the simple joy of eating together.

12:30
Wind-Down & Storytime

Cozy books and soft voices help us transition from a busy morning to a restful afternoon.

1:00
Rest & Recharge

Quiet time and naps for those who need them. Little bodies and big imaginations grow while they rest.

2:40
Snack & Reflection

One more snack, and a gentle look back on our day, what we made, learned, and loved most.

3:00
Home Time

Hugs, art to carry home, and happy goodbyes at 3:00 PM. Need more time? Extended care runs until 5:30 PM.

✦ Good to know

Preschool questions, answered

A few of the things parents ask us most. Don't see yours? We'd love to chat.

What ages do you welcome?+

Joyful Zen Preschool is for little ones ages 2½ to 5. We're currently enrolling 2 to 5 year olds in small, nurturing groups.

What are your hours?+

We're open Monday through Friday, 9:00am to 5:30pm. The core preschool day runs 9:00am to 3:00pm, with a half-day option from 9:00am to 12:00pm. Need a little more time? Extended care is available from 3:00pm to 5:30pm.

What's the teacher-to-child ratio?+

Just 1 teacher for every 4 children. Small groups mean every little one is truly seen, known, and cared for.

What's your educational philosophy?+

We're play-based, and Montessori and Reggio inspired. Days are built around safe, nurturing, developmentally-thoughtful activities that follow each child's curiosity. Both Spanish and English are part of everyday life here.

Does my child need to be potty-trained?+

Yes, children should be potty-trained before starting with us.

What does a typical day look like?+

Mornings begin with Montessori lessons and arts and crafts, followed by an organic brunch and a group mindfulness and yoga moment. From there it's STEAM and gardening projects, lunch, a restful nap, and an afternoon snack, before enrichment like soccer, music, and free-choice play to round out the day.

Do I need to tour before enrolling?+

Yes, and we wouldn't have it any other way. A tour comes before any application so you can feel the space, meet our team, and picture your child here.

How do I apply?+

Four simple steps: explore the program, schedule a tour, submit your enrollment application, then receive your invitation and complete the enrollment paperwork. We'll guide you through each one.
