The things parents ask us

Everything you might be wondering about life at the Academy. Still curious? We'd love to chat.

What's your educational philosophy?+

The years between 6 and 10 are where so much takes root. We keep that natural spark for learning alive with Montessori-inspired materials and hands-on discovery, so every child grows cognitively, socially, emotionally, and physically, in a space that feels safe, calm, and full of love.

Why a micro-school?+

Smaller means more, more attention, more flexibility, more of a learning home that actually fits your child. With so few students, the day can flex to who they are, parents stay close to the journey, and kids feel a steady sense of belonging and security.

What ages do you teach?+

Right now we welcome students from 1st through 5th grade. Our pod-based program is built to grow right alongside your child, expanding through elementary and into junior high (8th grade) as they do.

How big are the classes?+

Ten students, max, and often a few less. That's the whole point: a room small enough that every child is truly known, and every teacher has the room to meet them where they are.

What does the curriculum cover?+

A wonderfully full one: language, math, science, and social studies, woven together with art, mindfulness, philanthropy, entrepreneurship, group projects, and plenty of movement. We teach the whole child, head and heart.

How does each child get individual attention?+

It comes naturally with classes this small. All day long, our instructors tune into each child's pace and learning style, so the support, the challenge, and the encouragement always fit the kid in front of them.

What activities are on offer?+

Our afternoon activities kick off at 2pm with something for everyone, soccer, hip hop dance, martial arts, chess, cooking, Spanish, and more. They're a joyful way for kids to discover new passions (and have a blast doing it).

Is there a summer program?+

Yes! Our Summer Camp returns in July 2026, a season of core memories in the making, packed with the kind of experiences your child will hold onto for a lifetime.

Are meals and snacks provided?+

We serve a morning and afternoon snack, think organic fruits, veggies, crackers, yogurt, and other good stuff. Kids bring lunch from home, and we've got refrigerators and warming on hand to keep it fresh and cozy.

How do you keep kids safe?+

Your child's safety comes first, always. We have full fire and emergency protocols in place, our instructors are CPR-certified and trained, and our school sits in a secure facility, so you can relax knowing they're in good hands.

How do tours work?+

Tours are by appointment, usually Saturday mornings or afternoons. Bring your child along, it's the best way for them to feel the space and for you to picture them here. You'll meet our founder, Charlotte Acevedo, and have all the time you need to ask questions.

Do you offer payment plans?+

We don't offer financial aid at this time, but we're happy to work with you on flexible payment scheduling. Just ask, we'll find an arrangement that works.
