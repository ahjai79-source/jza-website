# Google Business Profile (GBP) Setup — Joyful Zen

Two **separate** profiles, one per location/brand. GBP is the #1 lever for local ("near me") ranking and the Map Pack. Do these in order; most of the ranking happens once the profile is **verified + complete + getting reviews**.

> ⚠️ Name rule: use the **real business name only** — do NOT add keywords like "Preschool Burbank" into the name field. Google suspends profiles for this. Keywords go in the *description, services, and posts*.

## Photos (both profiles) — aim for 15–25 at launch, add a few weekly
Google favors fresh, real photos. Upload:
- **Logo** (square) and **cover** (the tree mark / a warm classroom shot)
- Exterior + entrance (helps Google verify and helps parents find you)
- 6–10 interior/classroom shots
- Kids engaged in activities (faces only with photo consent)
- Teachers/staff, the founder
- Name each file descriptively before upload, e.g. `joyful-zen-academy-burbank-classroom.jpg`

## Google Posts — post 1×/week (keeps profile "active" = ranking signal)
Rotate these types. Keep ~150–300 chars + 1 image + a button (Book / Call / Learn more):
1. **Enrollment:** "Now enrolling ages 6–10 for 2026–27 in Burbank! Just 6 kids per teacher. Book a tour →"
2. **Event/urgency:** "New school year starts August 2026 — spots are limited. Schedule your visit this week."
3. **Behind-the-scenes:** a program highlight (chess club, swim day, market day) with a photo.
4. **Preschool drop-in:** "Teacher work day? Book an on-demand drop-in for ages 2½–5 →" (preschool profile)
5. **Review nudge / testimonial:** reshare a 5-star quote.

## Q&A — seed your own (post questions from your account, then answer)
- "What ages do you serve?" / "Where are you located?" / "Do you accept charter funds?" / "Do you offer drop-in or part-time care?" / "What is your student-teacher ratio?" / "What are your hours and is extended care available?"

## Reviews — the single biggest ranking + conversion factor
- Goal: **5–10 reviews in the first 2 weeks**, then a steady trickle. Ask happy current families first.
- Make a short link (GBP → "Get more reviews" gives you a link) and text/email it.
- **Request template (text/email):**
  > Hi [Name]! We're so glad [Child] is part of the Joyful Zen family 💛 Would you mind sharing a quick review of your experience? It helps other Burbank families find us. It takes 30 seconds: [review link]. Thank you!
- **Reply to every review** within a day or two (a few words + a warm thank-you; mention "Burbank" naturally once in a while). Google rewards owner responses.

## Beyond GBP — fast local wins (in priority order)
1. **Consistent NAP** (Name, Address, Phone) identical everywhere: GBP, website footer, and directories. Mismatches hurt ranking.
2. **Citations / listings:** Yelp, Bing Places, Apple Maps (Apple Business Connect), Nextdoor, GreatSchools, Niche, local parenting groups/FB.
3. **Backlinks:** local mom blogs, Burbank/Toluca Lake community sites, the chamber of commerce, partner orgs (BUMO).
4. **A few reviews mentioning each city** ("...we drive from Studio City...") gently broaden your geographic relevance.
5. Submit the **sitemap** (`/sitemap.xml`) in Google Search Console once the domain is live, and request indexing for each page.

## Realistic timeline
On-page SEO (done) + a complete, verified GBP + 5–10 reviews can land you in the local Map Pack for mid-tail terms ("micro school Burbank", "play-based preschool Burbank") within a few weeks. The broad head terms ("preschool near me") are more competitive and build over 1–3 months as reviews and citations accumulate. Reviews + weekly posts are what move it fastest.
