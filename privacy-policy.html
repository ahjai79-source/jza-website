Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Joyful Zen Academy ("we," "us," or "our") respects your family's privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've kept it short and plain on purpose.

Information we collect

When you fill out a tour request, admissions, or contact form, we collect the details you choose to share, typically your name, email address, phone number, your child's age, and any message you send us. We may also collect basic, non-identifying analytics about how visitors use our site (such as pages viewed) to help us improve it.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, schedule tours, share admissions details, and communicate with you about our programs. We do not sell your personal information, and we don't use it for advertising.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for use by parents and guardians, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through this site. Information about a child is provided by a parent or guardian and is used solely to support enrollment and communication with your family.

How we share information

We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate our site and respond to you (for example, form and email tools), and only as needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data security & retention

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, and we keep it only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or as required for our records.

Your choices

You may ask us to access, update, or delete the personal information you've shared at any time. To make a request, just reach out using the contact details below.
